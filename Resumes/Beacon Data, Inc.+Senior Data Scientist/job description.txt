Senior Data Scientist
Salt Lake City, Utah (Remote)
JOB LOCATION
This is a fully remote position (U.S.-based only).



THE ROLE: SENIOR DATA SCIENTIST

We're looking for a Senior Data Scientist to lead complex client engagements, design end-to-end analytics and machine learning solutions, and mentor other data scientists. This role blends deep technical expertise with strong client leadership—you'll own problem framing, solution design, and delivery while helping clients understand what to do next with their data.





ABOUT BEACON DATA

As a full-stack data and growth consulting firm, Beacon leverages machine and human intelligence to help clients achieve breakthrough performance. Organizations don't just call us to architect smart data strategy – they call us to implement it. As their expert data partner, our cross-functional team parachutes in to optimize data environments, unify fragmented data, and operationalize AI/ML use cases that accelerate growth. At Beacon, impact goes beyond improving the bottom line: one in four of our clients are mission-based organizations.





BEACON PROJECTS

No Beacon project is ever the same; we work across multiple sectors, providing unique learning and leadership opportunities. Examples include:

Stitching together tens of millions of airline customer records to boost loyalty program enrollment
Predicting optimal inventory levels for a supply chain firm to reduce idle time
Optimizing donor targeting for a leading children's hospital through advanced segmentation
Architecting and implementing a modern data strategy for the largest online theater ticketing company
Using ML/AI to optimize contact strategies and improve credit card debt recovery outcomes




WHAT YOU'LL DO

Lead discovery and exploratory analysis phases, including feature feasibility, data gaps, and tradeoff decisions that inform scope lock and delivery plans.
Lead the design and delivery of advanced analytics and machine learning solutions for client business problems
Translate ambiguous business questions into clear analytical approaches, models, and roadmaps
Partner directly with client stakeholders (including senior leaders) to guide decisions and recommend next steps
Partner with data engineering and DevOps to deploy scoring pipelines and APIs within client-controlled environments.
Design explainable scoring outputs and reason codes that support operational adoption and executive trust.
Lead recurring client meetings, clearly explaining technical concepts, tradeoffs, and results
Work hands-on with data across the full lifecycle: extraction, modeling, analysis, experimentation, and production deployment
Collaborate with data engineers, analytics engineers, and designers in a multidisciplinary, agile environment
Build reusable data science assets, tools, and patterns that strengthen Beacon Data's internal capabilities
Review code, provide technical guidance, and mentor junior team members
Support experimentation and measurement efforts such as A/B testing and performance evaluation
